# SIRPAM - Product Overview

## Project Purpose
SIRPAM is a luxury interior design / home décor brand website. It presents the brand's aesthetic identity, showcases products/gallery, and allows customers to customize their orders online.

## Key Features
- **Hero Section** – Full-screen brand landing with visual storytelling
- **Promise Section** – Brand values and quality commitments
- **Gallery Section** – Curated product/portfolio showcase
- **Testimonials Section** – Customer social proof
- **Customization Page** – Interactive product customization tool with sliders and options
- **Contact/Inquiry Flow** – Backend-connected form submission (Express + MongoDB)

## Target Users
- Prospective customers browsing luxury home décor
- Clients wanting to customize bespoke products
- Brand stakeholders reviewing the online presence

## Value Proposition
A premium, visually rich web experience that communicates craftsmanship, exclusivity, and personalization — matching the luxury positioning of the SIRPAM brand.
